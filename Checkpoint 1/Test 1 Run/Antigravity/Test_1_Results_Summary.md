# Test Case 1: Antigravity Run Summary

This document summarizes the results of the Phase 1 "Gold Standard" pipeline test executed locally by the Antigravity Orchestrator, Code Agent, and Validator Agent.

## The Goal
To autonomously replicate the correlation found in the *Health of the U.S. Nonprofit Sector 2025* report regarding Job Benefits and Staff Recruitment Impact using the `YEAR-04-DATA-PUF.csv` dataset.

## The Results
The pipeline successfully flattened the data, applied strict Likert scale mappings, and utilized the `year4wt` statistical survey weights to ensure an accurate representation of the data. The Validator Agent programmatically confirmed the absence of nulls and corrupted data strings before allowing the outputs to be finalized.

**Key Mathematical Finding:**
* **76.4%** of organizations reporting a *positive* impact on staff recruitment offer group health insurance.
* **16.5%** of organizations reporting a *negative* impact offer group health insurance.

These weighted findings successfully matched the 76% vs 18% correlation cited in the original research report.
